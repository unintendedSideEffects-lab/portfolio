Creative technologist, program strategist, and lifelong tinkerer. Edmonton-based. Roots go deep.

who I am

I'm an Indigenous woman building things at the intersection of data, systems, and people. My work lives in the space where community meets strategy — where a well-built spreadsheet can change how a program serves people, and where thoughtful design can make complex information feel human.

I've spent years in Indigenous economic development — administering federal grant programs, building reporting systems for multi-million-dollar loan portfolios, and turning raw data into stories that matter to funders, partners, and the communities being served. Now I bring that same precision and care to everything I build.

I also build things for the joy of it — websites, automations, AI-powered tools, art. If something I need doesn't exist yet, I'll make it myself.

I'm based in amiskwaciy-wâskahikan — what settlers call Edmonton — on Treaty 6 territory.
